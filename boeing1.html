Boeing : CCS-GPU STE

June 15th, 2022

The Common Core System (CCS) is often referred to as the “central nervous system and brain” of the airplane and hosts the aircraft’s avionics and utilities functions, eliminating several boxes and reducing hundreds of pounds of wire. Ground Processing Unit Special Testing Equipment ( GPU- STE) is responsible for analysing Aircraft Avionic System Health primarily for Lateral and Rudder Controls.

GPU-STE is designed, developed and produced in compliance of CCS involving Board Bring Up, PWB development , Thermal Optimisation concluding with features vix. HW Loopback, 70% Reusability projection, ARINC 664 GUI Analyser Integration, System Analysis with 35ms Resolution .

Overview

  • Testing HW for Aircraft Avionics
  • Project Duration: 8 months.
  • Productivity Savings: $25k (US).
  • Departments: Production, Quality Compliance, Design and Engineering.
